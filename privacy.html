Privacy Policy

How MedLab Reference collects, uses, and protects information when you use this site.

Last updated: July 13, 2026

Overview

MedLab Reference ("we," "us," "this site") is a free reference tool for medical laboratory test information. This policy explains what information is collected when you browse or use the site's features, and how it's used.

Information we collect

Account information (optional). If you create an account to save custom reference ranges, we use Firebase Authentication to store your email address and display name. We do not see or store your password directly — authentication is handled by Google Firebase.

Test suggestions. If you submit a test suggestion through the "Suggest a Test" form, the details you provide (test name, category, notes, and an optional contact email) are stored in our Firebase Firestore database so we can review and act on it.

Locally stored preferences. This site uses your browser's localStorage — a technology similar to cookies — to remember things like your light/dark theme preference and any custom reference ranges you've personally saved. This data stays on your device and is not automatically transmitted to us unless you're signed in and syncing.

Contact form. The contact form on this site opens your own email application with a pre-filled message; it does not transmit your message through our servers. If you email us directly, we retain that correspondence to respond to you.

Cookies and similar technologies

We do not use third-party advertising or tracking cookies on this website. We do not run analytics scripts or ad networks on the web version of MedLab Reference. The only client-side storage used is localStorage, described above, which is used strictly for site functionality (theme preference and saved reference ranges) rather than tracking or advertising.

How we use your information

  • To let you sign in and access saved, personalized reference ranges across visits
  • To review and respond to test suggestions or corrections you submit
  • To respond to messages sent through the contact form or email
  • To maintain and improve the accuracy and usability of the site

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Third-party services

This site uses Google Firebase (Authentication and Firestore database) to provide account and data-sync features. Firebase's handling of data is governed by Google's own privacy policy. No advertising SDKs are used on the website version of this service.

Your choices

  • You can use the site fully without creating an account — browsing and searching tests requires no personal information.
  • You can clear locally stored preferences at any time by clearing your browser's site data.
  • You can request deletion of your account or submitted data by contacting us at rahat119official@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "last updated" date.

Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to rahat119official@gmail.com or through our Contact page.
